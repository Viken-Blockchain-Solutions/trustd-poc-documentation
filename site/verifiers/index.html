Documentation for Verifiers in the Trustd Ecosystem

This document outlines the roles and verification processes for three key entities within the Trustd proof of concept (POC) for the Transport and Logistics industry, leveraging the Trustd.net platform for the issuance, holding, and verification of Verifiable Credentials (VCs).

Introduction

In the Trustd ecosystem, verifiers play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity and trustworthiness of the network by ensuring the authenticity and validity of issued VCs. This documentation focuses on the specific responsibilities and verification activities of three types of verifiers: Port Authority Compliance Departments, Freight Brokerage Firms, and Transportation and Logistics Inspectors.

Verifiers Overview

Port Authority Compliance Department

Role: Ensures compliance with safety, insurance, and operational regulations for all transport and logistics companies within the port's jurisdiction.

Verification Purpose: To confirm valid insurance and adherence to safety standards for vehicles operating within the port, minimizing risks and liabilities.

Process: Utilizes the Trustd.net platform to verify the authenticity of VCs, such as insurance certificates, issued to transport companies.

Freight Brokerage Firms

Role: Facilitates cargo shipments by acting as intermediaries between shippers and carriers.

Verification Purpose: To ensure contracted carriers possess valid insurance credentials and comply with industry regulations, maintaining service quality and mitigating transportation risks.

Process: Conducts verification of carriers' insurance VCs through the Trustd.net platform, streamlining the contracting process.

Transportation and Logistics Inspectors

Role: Conducts audits and inspections to enforce compliance with laws, safety standards, and environmental regulations in the transport and logistics sector.

Verification Purpose: To authenticate VCs such as insurance certificates and safety compliance credentials during audits, promoting industry standards.

Process: Employs the Trustd.net platform for efficient verification of VCs, ensuring a transparent and trustworthy inspection process.
